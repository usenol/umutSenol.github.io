Dessert

Ah, desserts—the grand finale to any meal, and at our hotel, we take this course seriously, crafting it into a memorable experience for all our guests. Our dessert offerings are a sumptuous blend of tradition, innovation, and pure indulgence, each prepared with the finest ingredients and an eye for artistic presentation. Our dessert menu features a wide array of choices, from classic confections to contemporary creations. Whether you crave the rich, deep flavors of a chocolate ganache tart, the light and airy delight of a mousse, or the refreshing zest of a citrus sorbet, our menu caters to every palate. For those who love to sample a little bit of everything, our dessert buffet is a dream come true. Featuring an array of bite-sized delights, from petit fours to chocolate truffles, the buffet is a feast for both the eyes and the palate. Special live dessert stations, like our flambeed desserts or freshly spun cotton candy, add an element of drama and spectacle to the dining experience.
