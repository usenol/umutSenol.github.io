Vegetarian and Vegan Options

At our hotel, we are committed to accommodating the dietary preferences and needs of all our guests, which is why our menus include a wide range of vegetarian and vegan options. From creatively crafted salads and hearty vegetable stews to innovative plant-based entrees and desserts, each dish is prepared using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to ensure a delicious and nutritious dining experience. Whether you are dining in our main restaurant, lounging by the pool, or enjoying room service, you'll find flavorful vegetarian and vegan dishes designed to satisfy and delight, ensuring that every guest, regardless of their dietary preferences, has a memorable and enjoyable meal.
